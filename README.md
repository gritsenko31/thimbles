## ğŸ® How to Play

### Objective
Watch the cups shuffle and remember which one hides the ball. Click the correct cup to win!

### Controls

**Desktop & Mobile:**
- **Click/Tap Cup** - Choose where you think the ball is
- **Place Bet** - Select bet amount before round starts
- **Start Button** - Begin shuffling
- **New Game** - Reset and start over

### Game Rules
- Ball is placed under one of three cups
- Cups shuffle rapidly for several seconds
- Keep your eyes on the cup with the ball
- Click the correct cup after shuffling stops
- Correct guess = win (2x your bet or point)
- Wrong guess = lose bet/streak
- Difficulty increases with each successful round

### Difficulty Levels
- **Easy** - Slow shuffles, 5-7 moves
- **Medium** - Moderate speed, 8-12 moves
- **Hard** - Fast shuffles, 12-15 moves
- **Expert** - Very fast, 15+ complex moves
